Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

Auto Tuck Shop helps tuck shops record sales through WhatsApp. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how shop owners can contact us about their data.

Information we collect

We may collect phone numbers, shop names, WhatsApp messages sent to the service, product and sales information, language preferences, and operational records needed to run the service.

How we use information

We use this information to register shops, process WhatsApp text and voice messages, record sales, send confirmations and reports, support shop operators, protect the service, and improve reliability.

Service providers

Auto Tuck Shop uses trusted providers for WhatsApp messaging, hosting, AI parsing, voice transcription, media storage, and error monitoring. These providers process information only as needed to deliver the service.

Data retention

We keep shop and sales records for as long as needed to provide the service, support shop owners, meet operational requirements, and resolve disputes. Shop owners can request deletion as described on the Data Deletion page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Auto Tuck Shop team through the support channel provided to your shop.
