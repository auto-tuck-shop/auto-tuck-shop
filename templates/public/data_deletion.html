Policy

Data Deletion

Last updated: 25 May 2026

Shop owners can ask Auto Tuck Shop to delete account and shop data associated with their phone number or shop.

How to request deletion

Contact the Auto Tuck Shop team through the support channel provided to your shop. Include the phone number and shop name connected to the account so we can verify the request.

What may be deleted

Deletion may include shop profile information, user profile information, WhatsApp message history, media references, product records, and sales records associated with the shop.

What may be retained

Some records may be retained for a limited time when required for security, fraud prevention, legal obligations, backups, or operational audit trails.

Processing time

We will review deletion requests and respond through the support channel used for the request.
