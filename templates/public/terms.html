Terms of Service

Last updated: 25 May 2026

These terms describe the basic rules for using Auto Tuck Shop, a WhatsApp-based sales recording service for tuck shops.

Using the service

Shop owners and approved assistants may use Auto Tuck Shop to send sales, product, and operational messages through WhatsApp. Users are responsible for sending accurate information and reviewing confirmations.

Accounts and access

Access may be approved, limited, or removed by the Auto Tuck Shop team to protect shop data, prevent misuse, or keep the service reliable.

Service availability

We aim to keep Auto Tuck Shop reliable, but messaging, transcription, AI parsing, hosting, and network providers may occasionally be delayed or unavailable.

Business records

Auto Tuck Shop helps create sales records from WhatsApp messages, but shop owners remain responsible for checking records and making business decisions.

Contact

For questions about these terms, contact the Auto Tuck Shop team through the support channel provided to your shop.
